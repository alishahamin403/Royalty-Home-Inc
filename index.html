Our Story

Toronto's Standard
for Luxury Living

Founded in Toronto, Royalty Home Inc. has spent over a decade transforming residential spaces across the GTA — from Forest Hill to the Beaches, Rosedale to Etobicoke.

We believe renovation is an act of care — for the home, for the people who live in it, and for the craftsmanship that will outlast trends. Every project begins with listening, and ends with a space you'll never want to leave.
